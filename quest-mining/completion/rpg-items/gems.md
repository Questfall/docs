# Gems

Gems are a planned RPG item type. They are intended to connect item rarity evolution with liquidity support, but they are not part of the current live RPG item loop yet.

## Current Status

| Surface | Status |
| --- | --- |
| Gem drops from lootboxes | Not intended |
| Gem rewards from liquidity program | Planned |
| Clothing rarity evolution with Gems | Not live |
| Gem marketplace strategy | Planned |

The live crafting loop currently supports item scrapping and clothing level-up. Clothing rarity evolution is not live yet.

## Intended Role

Gems are intended to be the scarce limiter for raising the rarity of levelled items such as clothing.

Business role:

* keep high-rarity item production scarce;
* connect crafting demand to liquidity support;
* give liquidity-focused players a future item reward path;
* let strong clothing become more valuable without replacing the whole item.

## How Gems Fit The Future Crafting Loop

The intended future loop is:

```text
support liquidity
-> earn Gem-related reward position
-> receive Gems
-> use Gems plus Essence to raise clothing rarity
-> produce stronger or more valuable clothing
```

The [Crafting Rarity](../rpg-attributes/crafting.md#rarity) and [Crafting Quality](../rpg-attributes/crafting.md#quality) traits are designed for this future loop.

Until Gems and rarity evolution are live, players should treat Gems as planned economy infrastructure, not as current inventory items.
